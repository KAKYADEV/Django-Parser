YouParse is a simple tool that analyzes web pages and helps you understand their SEO condition.

It allows you to quickly check how well a page is structured and identify potential issues that may affect visibility in search engines.

{{ header.1 }}

Using YouParse is straightforward:

  1. Enter the URL of the page you want to analyze.
  2. The system processes the page and extracts key data.
  3. You receive a structured report with the results.

The goal is to give you useful insights without overwhelming you with technical details.

{{ header.2 }}

YouParse focuses on the most important elements of a web page:

  • Meta tags (title, description)
  • Headings (H1, H2, etc.)
  • Images and their attributes
  • Internal and external links

{{ header.3 }}

SEO score is a simplified metric that represents the overall quality of a page from an SEO perspective.

It is based on the presence and structure of key elements such as metadata, headings, and content organization.

The score is not absolute, but it gives you a quick way to evaluate and compare pages.
